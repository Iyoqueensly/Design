About me

I struggled with OChem mechanisms until I found a way to draw them as little stories. Happy to share the framework that finally made it click.

I tutor in English and German, and I'm flexible about meeting on campus at the library or jumping on a Zoom call. For exam prep weeks I block out evenings — book early if you want one of those slots.

Outside of tutoring I work two days a week at the Krems hospital lab, which is where I first got obsessed with organic chemistry. Ask me about it, it's actually fun once you get past the arrow-pushing.
